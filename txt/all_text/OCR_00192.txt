**Description of Proposed Project:**
The proposed project involves constructing a bank-style river water intake structure consisting of a pre-press intake vault with a flat intake screen on the North Saskatchewan River (NSR) near Frenchman's Butte. The river intake will connect to a pipeline that will supply water to the Glenbow Thermoelectric Thermal.

**Description of Authorized Work(s), undertaking(s) or activity(ies):**
The work, s(understanding), or activities associated with the proposed project described above are likely to result in serious harm to fish

- The installation and operation of a bank-style river water intake structure consisting of steel sheet piles and a pre-cast concrete intake vault located on the north bank of the NSR.
  
**Conditions of Authorization:**

**Section 2. Conditions that relate to measures and standards to avoid and mitigate serious harm to fish**

- **Subsection: Sediment and erosion control**
  - Sediment and erosion control measures must be in place and shall be upgraded and maintained, such that release of sediment is avoided at the location of the authorized work(s), undertaking(s), or activity(ies).

    - An Erosion and Sediment Control (ESC) plan shall be developed for the site that avoids sediment from entering the NSR during all phases of the project. Erosion and Sediment Control measures must be maintained until all disturbed ground have been permanently stabilized to ensure that suspended soil particles in surface runoff water are trapped before being discharged into the aquatic environment.

    - Effective sediment control is installed prior to construction and inspected, maintained and repaired as required;

- **Subsection: During in-water activities**
  - In-water environmental monitors will provide mitigation measures during an on-site environmental monitor; a work site staff if unexpected sources;
  
- **Subsection: Implementation of a TSS/terbium program**
  - Monitor sediment downstream of the work area during sheet pile installation;

- **Subsection: Minimize clearing of riparian vegetation.**
  - Disturbed areas within the temporary construction footprints shall be re-planted and exposed soils and river banks stabilized as appropriate.
