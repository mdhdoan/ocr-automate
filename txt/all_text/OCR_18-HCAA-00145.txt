**Description of Proposed Projected Site:**
The proposed project of which the work, undertaking or activity authorized is a part involves:

- The removal of two existing Highway 401 bridge structures crossing the Grand River near Kitchener, ON and their replacement with four long span bridge structures with a 40-55-76.5 m span configuration. Additionally, the extension of two corrugated steel pipe culverts located on an unnamed tributary to Blair Creek and an unnamed tributary to the Grand River.

**Description of Authorized Work(s), undertaking(s) or activity(ies):**
likely to result in serious harm to fish
